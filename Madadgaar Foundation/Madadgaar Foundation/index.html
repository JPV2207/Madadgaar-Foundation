Our Team

Ananya Sharma
Founder
Meet Ananya Sharma, the visionary founder of Madadgaar Foundation. As a dedicated women entrepreneur, Ananya's passion lies in transforming lives across various domains - women empowerment, animal welfare, and child education. With unwavering determination, she established the Madadgaar Foundation, driven by the belief that dreams should not remain unfulfilled. Through her organization, Ananya is on a mission to create a new world of possibilities for those in need. Join her in this noble journey as together, we can make a difference and bring positive change to countless lives.
Raj Rathaur
Co-Founder
Raj Rathaur is a highly adept juggler, the co-founder of Madadgaar Foundation and the founder of Career Corps with the motive to grow together with others and for others. He is a BCA graduate from Shri Ramswaroop Memorial College of Engineering and Management, Lucknow. He is an enthusiastic entrepreneur who has had immense success in managing his communities. He is highly skilled at community building and aspires to leverage his cognitive curiosity to build tech-powered solutions in the field of education and social good. He is zealous about creating successful organizations that impact society for the better. He is determined towards entrepreneurship and always looking for the best ways to achieve what he sets out.
